Badge

Badges are small circles, positioned either at top-right or bottom-right of the parent component. Badge can be used to display numbers, online / offline status, depending on where they are used.

Badge on Avatars

Profile Badge is used to show the current status of a user. It can be used with 3 variants : online, offline and dnd

Badge on Icons

Badge is used to show the no. of items present inside component.
